SHERLOCK

Established in 2023, Sherlock bookstore's aim is to create a community of book lovers throughout the nation, especially those who favor crime/detective novels. There, not only can customers browse for books, but they can also become a member of the community, where they can build the reading habit and share feelings through each page of book.
